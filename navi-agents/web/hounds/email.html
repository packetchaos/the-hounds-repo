GABRIEL

Email & Reports · the loop-closer
Gabriel Hound · Category: Loop / Reporting
“Findings that reach the right human.”

Gabriel is the messenger — the one who carries the word to those who need it. It takes what the whole pack found and delivers it to the humans who own it, with deep-links back into the Tenable platform, so a finding doesn't just get tagged — it gets acted on.

THE PROBLEM

The pack can find, prioritize and tag exposure all day — but exposure only shrinks when a human fixes it. Most programs stall at the last mile: a dashboard nobody opens, a CSV nobody routes, a KEV alert buried in a channel. The loop stays open.

WHAT IT DOES

Gabriel turns the pack's findings into email that lands. It reads Atlas Owner: tags and sends each owner only their assets; fires KEV alarms and cert countdowns; sends a leadership morning briefing; and gives every Hound a one-click way to email its findings — the detection signals, the tagged assets, and Tenable deep-links — labelled with which Hound produced them.

KEY CAPABILITIES

  • Owner-routed remediation — reads Atlas Owner : <group/user> tags and emails each owner a personalized list of their assets, each with its Tenable asset link and top findings.
  • Tenable deep-links — every asset carries its platform URL; the technical template lists each finding's plugin deep-link, plus a tag filter link to view the whole set in-platform.
  • KEV fire-alarms & cert countdowns — targeted alerts from Laelaps (actively-exploited) and Certania (expiring certs), not noisy digests.
  • Morning briefing — exposure-at-a-glance for leadership, on the Contract cadence.
  • Agent findings hand-off — the pack's shared second option: any Hound sends Gabriel the finding IDs that made up its asset search + the matched assets + the tag it applied, and Gabriel emails the overview.
  • Board vs technical templates — a summary for leadership, per-asset detail for the people who fix it.

HOW IT WORKS

Gabriel reads the local navi.db (owners, assets, findings, certs — all read-only) to compose and preview every email. Sending goes through navi action mail and is double-gated: it needs writes enabled and a separate email opt-in (NAVI_EMAIL=1) and an explicit confirm. SMTP is configured once, out-of-band. Every send is logged as an accountability ledger — who was notified, when.

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT

  • Closes the loop — most tools stop at "here's the finding"; Gabriel puts it in the owner's inbox with a link to fix it.
  • Personalized by ownership — each owner gets only their assets, which is what makes the outreach land instead of getting ignored.
  • Preview-first, double-gated — you see exactly what goes out; nothing sends without a separate email opt-in and a confirm.
  • Wired to the whole pack — every Hound can hand its findings to Gabriel in one click.

WORKS BETTER WITH

Gabriel pairs with Atlas (Owner tags drive the routing), Laelaps (KEV alarms), Certania (cert countdowns), Argos (per-asset dossiers to link) and the AI Contract (email the plan on arm, the result on finish).

WHO IT'S FOR

VM leads who need findings to reach a human; remediation owners who want just their slice; leadership who want a briefing, not a spreadsheet.

CALL TO ACTION

Let Gabriel carry the word — and finally close the loop.
