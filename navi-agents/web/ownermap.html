📉 The reduction — CVE → Plugin → App → Path

Most teams track CVEs — the most duplicated unit of work. But multiple CVEs roll up into one plugin (a single detection & fix), multiple plugins into one app / route (the owner-actionable unit), and each app spreads across many paths (where it lives). Climb the ladder and the backlog collapses: own a handful of apps instead of tens of thousands of CVEs.

Coverage gaps — Ownerless Risk

Ownerless Risk is exposure with nobody assigned to it — routes and paths hiding risk because no one is on the hook to fix them. Plus the under-owned surface (too few owners for all its assets). Assign these on the Ownership Assignment page.
